Home Remedies for Period Cramps

  • Warm Compress: Place a heating pad or hot water bottle on your lower abdomen to relax muscles and relieve pain.
  • Herbal Teas: Drink chamomile, ginger, or peppermint tea to help reduce cramps and soothe your body.
  • Gentle Exercise: Light activities like walking or yoga can increase blood flow and ease discomfort.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to help reduce bloating and ease cramps.
  • Massage: Gently massage your lower abdomen in circular motions to help relieve tension.
  • Healthy Diet: Eat foods rich in magnesium (like bananas, nuts, and leafy greens) to help relax muscles.
  • Rest: Make sure to get enough sleep and rest when you need it.
