Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are PetalFlow's predictions?
PetalFlow's AI algorithm learns from your unique cycle patterns over time. The more data you input, the more accurate our predictions become. Most users report high accuracy after 2-3 cycles of consistent tracking.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export your cycle data and insights at any time from the Settings page. This feature is useful for sharing information with your healthcare provider.
